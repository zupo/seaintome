A web-log of my sessions on the water.


I'm a geek who fell in love with windsurfing, and by extension, any form of surfing. I try to get out on the water twice every week and have been successful in doing so for a number of years. I used to primarily hunt wind but lately I'm all about waves.


If you are from the Adriatic region you might wanna join our "quick daily sessions" mailing list. You will receive regular forecast updates from totisurf.com and invites to go hunt wind at local spots.
